Famous and Best Places in Chandigarh

The Rock Garden Of Chandigarh

Gratitute to Nek Chand Ji who has created this master-peice which is a must viist to all tourist travelling to Chandigarh.
There is so many facet to this garden which takes a traveller to different zones.

Sukhna Lake

Water, Rain, Rivers, Lakes are divine for centuries in Indian culture. The Sukhna gets its water from "cho" running down from the Siwaliks (Himalayan foothills), through the Sukhna Wildlife Sanctuary.
There is a rock garden, walkers/joggers path, and a Garden of Silence.

Elante Mall

Great mall with lots of stores and restaurant options.
The entire top floor has several bars, restaurants, food court, arcade, and movie theatre.
